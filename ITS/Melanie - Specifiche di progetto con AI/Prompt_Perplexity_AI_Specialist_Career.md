# Prompt per Perplexity — AI Systems Engineer Career Path Analysis

**Scopo:** Identificare il mercato italiano per AI Systems Engineer (ruolo sostenibile long-term, non commoditizzabile) e capire il gap di skills.

**Dove eseguire:** Perplexity (modalità ricerca web ON)

---

## STEP 1 — TASK

I want to understand the market for "AI Systems Engineer" roles in Italy 
so that I can assess whether this specialization is more sustainable long-term than "Prompt Engineer"
and identify the exact skills/projects that make me hireable.

---

## STEP 2 — MY BACKGROUND (Context)

Currently completing an intensive AI bootcamp covering:
- **Prompting** (advanced frameworks: C.I.A.R.E., Chain-of-Thought, multi-agent boards, scenario simulation)
- **Automation** (GoHighLevel CRM, API design, webhooks, AI Agent orchestration)
- **Full-stack Programming** (Frontend: UI/UX design; Backend: architecture, database, API)
- **AI Project Management** (technical specs, prompt logging, incident tracking, MVP validation)
- **Business & Compliance** (EU AI Act 2024/1689, risk classification, product strategy)

**Key insight:** I realize "Prompt Engineer" is commoditizing (anyone asks ChatGPT better). I'm pivoting to **AI Systems Engineer** — who designs architectures, manages quality gates, orchestrates deployment, handles risk.

**Geographic**: Italy (EU regulations apply)
**Language**: Italian primary, English fluent
**Career stage**: Early career, completing bootcamp Q3 2026

---

## STEP 3 — REFERENCE: WHAT I MEAN BY "AI SYSTEMS ENGINEER"

This is **NOT** a Prompt Engineer. Comparison:

| Prompt Engineer | AI Systems Engineer |
|---|---|
| Writes better prompts | Designs problem + architecture |
| ChatGPT familiarity | System design + software engineering |
| Output: text | Output: deployable system |
| Easily automated | Requires human judgment |
| Commoditizing | Sustainable |

**AI Systems Engineer** = Software architect who specializes in AI/LLM systems. Orchestrates multi-agent systems, manages quality gates, handles deployment, cost optimization, risk mitigation.

## APPENDIX — Why This Pivot Matters

**Prompt Engineer is commoditizing because:**
- Anyone can ask ChatGPT better (no specialist needed)
- Companies will build this in-house (no external hire)
- Salary will compress to €25-35k in 2 years

**AI Systems Engineer is sustainable because:**
- Requires architectural thinking (not commoditizable)
- Needs software engineering discipline (hard to learn quickly)
- Shortage of people who can do this well
- Salary grows faster (€60-120k range long-term)

This prompt seeks to validate whether that pivot is real or wishful thinking.
