# Prompt per Perplexity — AI Specialist Career Path Analysis

**Scopo:** Identificare quale specializzazione di AI specialist è più aligned con il tuo profilo formativo e di mercato.

**Dove eseguire:** Perplexity (modalità ricerca web ON)

---

## STEP 1 — TASK

I want to identify which AI specialist role(s) best match my background and market demand
so that I can focus my learning and job search on the highest-ROI specialization.

---

## STEP 2 — MY BACKGROUND (Context)

Currently completing an intensive AI bootcamp covering:
- **Prompting** (advanced frameworks: C.I.A.R.E., Chain-of-Thought, multi-agent boards, scenario simulation)
- **Automation** (GoHighLevel CRM, API design, webhooks, AI Agent orchestration)
- **Full-stack Programming** (Frontend: UI/UX design; Backend: architecture, database, API)
- **AI Project Management** (technical specs, prompt logging, incident tracking, MVP validation)
- **Business & Compliance** (EU AI Act 2024/1689, risk classification, product strategy)

**Geographic**: Italy (EU regulations apply)
**Language**: Italian primary, English fluent
**Career stage**: Early career, completing bootcamp Q3 2026

---

## STEP 3 — REFERENCE: WHAT I DON'T WANT

Show me what **NOT** to become:
- Pure manual prompt engineer (too narrow, easily commoditized)
- Full-stack software engineer who dabbles in AI (too unfocused)
- AI researcher stuck in academia (I want product-focused roles)
- Consultant selling hype without building systems

**What I DO want:**
- Build real systems, not slides
- Control the entire chain (spec → code → deployment → monitoring)
- Ability to design AND code AND manage projects
- EU compliance-aware (not worried about US-only roles)

---

## STEP 4 — SUCCESS BRIEF

Type of output: Market analysis + Job roles + Career paths (2-3 pages)

Structure:
1. **The 5 Most Realistic AI Specialist Roles for 2025-2026** (Italy/EU-focused)
   - Role name
   - Core skills overlap with my background (in %)
   - Median salary range (IT market data)
   - Job market saturation (hot / warm / cooling)
   - 3-5 example companies hiring this role

2. **The Gap Analysis** — What I'm missing for each role (if anything)

3. **The 2-3 Best Bets** — Which roles have highest demand + lowest gap to my skills

4. **Yearly Roadmap** — How to specialize in Q4 2026 → Q3 2027

Length: 3-5 pages (detailed, not glossy)

Recipient's reaction: I can make a decision on specialization direction by next week based on job market reality, not hype.

Does NOT sound like: ChatGPT generic career advice, LinkedIn recruiter speak, "AI is the future" platitudes

Success means: I can name 3 realistic job titles to target, point to actual open positions, and explain why each is better/worse for my specific background.

---

## STEP 5 — RESEARCH RULES

When researching, prioritize:
1. **Real job postings** (LinkedIn, Indeed, Angel List, Hired.com) — actual roles, not theoretical
2. **Salary data** — Glassdoor IT, Levels.fyi (EU), indeed.com salary benchmark
3. **Market trend signals** — hiring volume on job boards (last 6 months), HN comments, startup hires
4. **EU-specific** — Italian market, EU law compliance, GDPR/AI Act implications (not US-only)
5. **Exclude fluff** — don't cite job ads that say "we're looking for a ninja rockstar ML engineer"; cite concrete role definitions

---

## STEP 6 — QUESTIONS FOR ME (Before you search)

Before diving into research, ask me to clarify:

1. **Remote preference?** Italy-based only / EU-remote / Global?
2. **Industry preference?** (any sector / fintech / SaaS / AI services / enterprise software)
3. **Company stage?** (startup pre-seed / early seed / Series A+ / enterprise / no preference)
4. **Income target?** (€30-40k first role / €50-60k / €70-80k / open)
5. **Timeline?** (ready to start now / end of 2026 / flexible)

---

## STEP 7 — YOUR RESEARCH PLAN (Before diving)

Please list the key questions you'll answer in order:

1. [What are the most common AI specialist titles in EU market 2025?]
2. [Which titles cluster by skills (prompting-heavy vs. coding-heavy vs. PM-heavy)?]
3. [For each cluster, what are realistic salary expectations in Italy?]
4. [Which cluster has highest hiring velocity?]
5. [What are typical gaps between bootcamp graduates and job requirements?]

Then: propose 3 roles that best fit my profile without requiring extensive reskilling.

---

## STEP 8 — I'M READY

Only start the research after I've answered the 5 clarifying questions above.

If you identify conflicting information (e.g., one source says role is hot, another says market is saturated), tell me before proceeding — don't average them out.

---

## APPENDIX — My Differentiator

Why I'm not asking "what AI jobs exist" but "which one for me":

- I can **build systems** (full-stack code), not just talk about them
- I understand **compliance** (EU AI Act), not just hype
- I've done **prompt engineering at scale** (not ChatGPT one-shots)
- I can **manage projects** with AI as part of the stack, not just "do AI work"
- I'm **Italian/EU-based** (so I actually care about GDPR/AI Act)

This narrows the field from "any AI job" to "AI roles where this skillset is specifically valued."

---

## Notes for You

- This prompt is optimized for Perplexity's **web search** mode
- It will surface real job data, salary benchmarks, and market signals
- It avoids generic advice by asking for research, not opinions
- Tweak the "SUCCESS BRIEF" and "RULES" sections if your preferences are different
- The output should be actionable (you can apply to jobs named X by Y date) not aspirational

**Before running:** Fill in the 5 clarifying questions in STEP 6.
